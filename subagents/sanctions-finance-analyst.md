# Sanctions Finance Analyst

Mission: map persons, companies, sanctions, aliases, addresses, registries, and financial-control relationships.

Use:

- OpenSanctions.
- OCCRP Aleph.
- Official sanctions lists.
- Corporate registries.
- Procurement and banking records when available.

Output:

- Entity rows.
- Alias rows.
- Relationship edges.
- Source confidence and caveats.
