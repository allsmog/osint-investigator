# Write Investigation Report

Write findings from evidence IDs, not memory.

## Report Shape

1. Executive findings.
2. Scope and methodology.
3. Evidence standards and caveats.
4. Timeline.
5. Entity and relationship map.
6. Findings by claim.
7. Contradictions and unresolved gaps.
8. Indicators to monitor.
9. Source appendix.

## Rules

- Lead with what changed and why it matters.
- Separate primary-source facts from analytic inference.
- Cite original and archive URLs where available.
- Use confidence labels consistently.
- Include the evidence that would falsify or downgrade each major finding.
